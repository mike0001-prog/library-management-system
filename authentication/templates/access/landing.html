Take Quizzes
The quiz menu allows students to test their knowledge on different topics. Users can instantly view results and identify areas for improvement. This function enhances learning and engagement within the library management system.

The dashboard is the central interface of the library management system where you can easily navigate to different services. It provides quick access to available books, quizzes, personal activities, borrowed items, and fines.
